[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
[Acoustic guitar strums softly, warm and intimate]
I trace the static on the midnight dial
Hoping the frequency will catch your smile
The dashboard glows and softly starts to frame
The way the radio still says your name

[Verse 1]
[Soft fingerpicked guitar, restrained drum beat]
I am driving past the tavern on the edge of town
Remembering the way you let your hair fall down
The leather seat is empty by my side
Just wishing you were here to share the ride

[Pre-Chorus]
[Bass line deepens, melodic tension rises]
The empty miles are stretching out of sight
But you are all I am thinking of tonight
I feel the lonely fire in my soul
I want your tender touch to take control

[Chorus]
[Smooth chorus lift, anthemic acoustic-first texture]
You are the rhythm in the quiet dark
The only melody that moves my heart
I close my eyes and feel you pull me near
The radio still says your name my dear

[Post-Chorus]
[Light piano accents with acoustic strumming]
Oh the midnight air is breathing out your song
I want you wrapped around me all night long

[Verse 2]
[Slightly heavier acoustic strum, emotional groove]
The streetlights paint a shadow on the dash
A memory of us in every flash
I roll the windows down to catch the breeze
But every whisper brings you back to me

[Bridge]
[Atmospheric reverb textures, emotional pause]
